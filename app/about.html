The Story of Flyby

Growing up, I was always very health-focused but once I entered college I found myself drinking a lot more. I also lacked an enzyme that allowed me to properly break down alcohol and this only made the problem worse. Desperate for a cure and a healthier way to drink, I started testing home remedies, but none of them worked.

On a trip to Tokyo, I found myself out in an all-you-can-drink izakaya with a few close friends. As we geared up for an adventurous evening, one of my friends suggested taking a Japanese drinking supplement. Intrigued, I decided to put the drink to the test – while taking full advantage of the bar’s bottomless beverage offerings.
